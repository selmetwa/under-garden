Under Garden

Under Garden is a tunneling/falling sand game build using Rust/WASM/HTML/CSS/JS. Inspired by Conways' Game of Life & Sandspeil. With code borrowed from both.

    Cells

  • Soil

    Used to plant seeds.

  • Water

    Waters seeds, and makes plants grow.

  • Dynamite

    Blows through other materials, creates steam if it comes in contact with water. Creates debris if it comes in contact with anything else. Stone can slow dynamite slightly.

  • Worms

    Travel in groups of 3-5. Eat through all materials but stone.

  • Seeds

    Used to create plants, must land in soil to turn into a plant.

  • Stone

    Blocks stuff.
